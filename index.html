Our Journey & Company History

ConnectoxHome Party Bus Rentals was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional transportation experiences that enhance celebrations and create lasting memories. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

2008 - The Beginning

ConnectoxHome was established with a single party bus and a vision to revolutionize group transportation. Our founder, recognizing a gap in the market for reliable, fun party bus services, launched the company with a focus on customer-centric service.

2010 - First Expansion

Due to overwhelming demand, we expanded our fleet to five vehicles and hired our first dedicated support staff. This period marked our transition from a startup to an established service provider.

2013 - Technology Integration

We invested in advanced booking systems and GPS tracking technology, allowing customers to monitor their vehicles in real-time and providing enhanced safety and accountability.

2015 - Regional Recognition

ConnectoxHome received multiple industry awards for customer service excellence and safety standards. Our reputation as a trusted provider continued to grow throughout the region.

2017 - Fleet Modernization

We completely modernized our fleet with state-of-the-art vehicles featuring the latest entertainment and safety technologies. This investment demonstrated our commitment to providing cutting-edge services.

2019 - Sustainability Initiatives

ConnectoxHome began implementing eco-friendly practices, including fuel-efficient vehicles and carbon offset programs, reflecting our commitment to environmental responsibility.

2021 - Digital Transformation

We launched our mobile app, allowing customers to book, track, and manage their reservations seamlessly. This digital transformation improved accessibility and customer convenience.

2023 - Present Day

Today, ConnectoxHome operates a fleet of 25+ vehicles and serves thousands of customers annually. We continue to innovate and expand our services while maintaining the core values that built our reputation.
